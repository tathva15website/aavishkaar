“Between the pages of a book, there is a lovely place to live in”. For those who haven’t lived between these pages we present Project Daisy in collaboration with Kerala federation of Blinds. We create the audio versions of notable works of literature in Malayalam and let those less gifted get a glimpse of the world within a book.

Today we live in this world with all happiness because of great sacrifices of our parents. But many of them live isolated from us now. We return their love by providing them amenities they avoided so that we could live with it now. We are installing solar powered water heater and setting up a Bio gas plant in an old age home in Kannur and giving them hope that they aren’t deserted after all.

Srishti is a colossal venture held as a part of social initiative Aavishkaar, Tathva. It is an attempt to kindle waves of changes and reforms in the social scenario through igniting minds and bringing out the hidden potential and creativity. This aspiration focuses on desires of talented minds to direct the society towards vistas of peace and luxury, bereft of the social plagues. The most promising ones are chosen and driven to reality. This is where skills meet inventions and ideas bring miracles. Srishti brings before you the mightiest opportunities to reveal your vision of a healthy society with your virtual thoughts. Through Srishti let us unite our dreams relished with our talents to mould the glorious future. GET READY TO TRANSFORM. THE WORLD HERE AWAITS YOU.....
